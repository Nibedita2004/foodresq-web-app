Rescue good food. Feed your community.

Food Rescue is a community-driven platform connecting donors with volunteers to redistribute surplus food quickly and safely. Create listings, claim pickups, and help reduce food waste in your area.

  • Fast listing creation
  • Volunteer pickup coordination
  • Simple and secure authentication

How it works

  1. Donor: Create a listing of surplus food.
  2. Volunteer: Claim the pickup and collect the donation.
  3. Complete: Mark pickup complete — food gets redistributed.
